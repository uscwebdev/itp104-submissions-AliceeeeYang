Project Summary

Topic:

The topic of my website is a Beijing traveling guide. It has four pages in total, a home page containing a summary of Beijing, a places page displaying the most famous places in Beijing to visit, a food page introducing the best restaurants in Beijing, and a resources page for people to use when traveling to Beijing. It serves a detailed travel guide for anyone who is interested in visiting Beijing

Purpose:

The purpose of my website is to introduce people to my hometown and my favorite city, Beijing. I want people to have a guide when they visit Beijing. I have been taking friends to travel in Beijing several times, and I want to make a personal guide with my favorite places, foods, and resources. I hope my website can help people to better enjoy their time in Beijing.

Extra:

The first extra I used is iframe. I put an iframe on every resource I listed on the website so people can directly interact with the website. The second extra I used is form. Many people may have questions about certain places or travel plans after visiting the website. I want to answer those questions to better assist them through the form.
